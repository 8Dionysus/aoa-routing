# Agon Gate Trigger Model

Wave V defines pre-protocol trigger classes.

A trigger is not an event in an arena. It is a routing-recognized pressure pattern that suggests ordinary handling may be insufficient.

## Trigger classes

- `closure`: closure is contested or premature
- `contradiction`: a material contradiction remains open
- `trace`: proof or closure depends on a broken trace
- `evidence`: the evidence floor collapses
- `failure`: repeated failure occurs without state delta
- `assistant`: a civil/service agent hits scope or anti-drift limits
- `model_divergence`: actor models or route interpretations diverge
- `canon`: ToS or source-canon risk appears
- `summon`: summon intent needs review
- `novelty`: novelty exceeds service handling
- `boundary`: actor kind or authority mismatch appears

## Trigger output

A trigger may yield:

- `agon_gate_candidate`
- `agon_gate_candidate_missing_context`
- `owner_review_required`
- `quarantine_hint`

It may not yield a live arena session.
