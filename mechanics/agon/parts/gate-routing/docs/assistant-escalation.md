# Agon Gate Assistant Escalation

Assistant civil actors may serve the routing gate.

They may not become hidden contestants.

## Assistant may

- flag scope breach
- request missing fields
- prepare a receipt
- preserve trace context
- hand off to an agonic actor or owner review surface

## Assistant may not

- become a contestant
- issue a verdict
- grant closure
- initiate summon
- write a scar
- mutate rank
- promote to `Tree-of-Sophia`
- rewrite persistent self-policy

## Gate effect

When an assistant crosses its scope boundary, routing should prefer explicit escalation over improvisation.

The assistant does not become stronger by hiding drift.
It becomes trustworthy by stopping cleanly.
