# Agon Parts

| Part | Role | Active home |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `gate-routing` | Pre-protocol Agon gate trigger, route hint, owner dispatch seam, and generated registry. | [parts/gate-routing](parts/gate-routing/README.md) |
| `recurrence-adapter` | Observation-only recurrence manifest for Agon gate surfaces. | [parts/recurrence-adapter](parts/recurrence-adapter/README.md) |

Parts are operation nodes. Their docs, schemas, examples, generated companions,
scripts, tests, and manifests stay with the part that owns the route. Durable
current quest records stay in root `quests/agon/`; historical receipts stay in
`legacy/raw/`.
