# Boundary Bridge Roadmap

## Current Contour

Boundary Bridge is present as federation entry routing, cross-repo bridge
orientation, owner-layer shortlist, federation harvest routing, ToS/KAG
boundary, and stats re-grounding hints.

It routes across owner boundaries without transferring authority.

## Next Work

- Keep federation entries and owner shortlists source-owned and advisory.
- Keep ToS/KAG boundary signals weaker than ToS canon and KAG source-lift
  owners.
- Keep stats re-grounding hints weaker than stats evidence and SDK control
  posture.

## When Time Comes

- Add new bridge parts only when a repeated cross-owner route cannot fit an
  existing part.
- Add owner acceptance receipts after sibling repositories land bridge support.

## Out Of Scope

- Source authority transfer.
- ToS canon writes.
- KAG promotion approval.
- Stats certification.
