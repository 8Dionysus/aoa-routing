# Governance Roadmap

## Current Contour

Governance is present as appeal and veto routing, charter change routing,
council dispatch, governance escalation, and precedent route hints.

The mechanic routes governance pressure to the owner that can decide. It does
not become the council, charter authority, or precedent source.

## Next Work

- Keep governance route signals explicit about owner and next route.
- Keep precedent hints advisory and source-referenced.
- Add tests only where route signals become schema-backed public outputs.

## When Time Comes

- Add owner-local governance receipts when a stronger owner accepts a routed
  decision lane.
- Add generated governance readers only after repeated escalation patterns need
  public routing lookup.

## Out Of Scope

- Charter mutation authority.
- Council decision authority.
- Binding precedent creation.
