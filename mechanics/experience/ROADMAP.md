# Experience Roadmap

## Current Contour

Experience is present as routing support for office route gates, service
handoff, adoption routing, and certification owner landing.

The mechanic names where experience-shaped pressure should go. It does not own
service policy, office installation, adoption truth, or certification authority.

## Next Work

- Keep office, service, adoption, and certification parts bounded to route
  shape and owner handoff.
- Update part contracts when an owner repo lands a concrete acceptance receipt.
- Keep experience route signals out of root docs unless they become public
  routing outputs.

## When Time Comes

- Add owner-local adoption receipts after sibling repos accept adoption routes.
- Add generated experience readers only if repeated route signals need a public
  routing surface.

## Out Of Scope

- Owner-local office setup.
- Service execution policy.
- Certification authority or reward logic.
