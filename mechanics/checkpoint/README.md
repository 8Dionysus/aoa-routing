# Checkpoint

This mechanic records routing-owned participation in OS Abyss checkpoint flows.

`aoa-routing` does not own checkpoint truth, continuity legitimacy, or budget
policy. It may publish bounded route-review surfaces that point to stronger
owners.

## Parts

- `live-session-reentry-route-review`: candidate-only route review surface for
  reviewed checkpoint or continuity reentry.

## Owner Split

- `aoa-memo` owns checkpoint carry and writeback meaning.
- `aoa-agents` owns continuity-run posture.
- `aoa-evals` owns replay and runtime integrity proof.
- `Agents-of-Abyss` owns center law and budget authority boundaries.
- `aoa-routing` owns the navigation envelope and validation that the route does
  not fall back to router-owned authority.
